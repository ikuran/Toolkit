Core Claims
Primary Claim
When form and typology are suspended and replaced with an agent-based prompt framework, designers from different disciplines independently converge on the same spatial logics.

How to Use the Toolkit
The parameters on the right are not just simulation controls — they are design decisions. Each slider corresponds to a spatial logic identified in the workshop. Click a step to expand.
You Are Inside the System
ABST does not ask you to draw a form. It asks you to configure the conditions — who the agents are, what forces act between them, how time flows. Form emerges from all of this. In Stage 2, your movement is a field parameter. In Stage 3, the field crystallises around you.
